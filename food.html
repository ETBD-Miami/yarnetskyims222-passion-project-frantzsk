Food

I am not a picky eater. When it comes to food, I like almost anything (except maybe oysters or clams--I've never tried them but they gross me out). I enjoy my mom's cooking, but without access to her recipes, I thought I would share my favorite restaurants to eat at. I apologize if these aren't close to you!

101 Beer Kitchen

101 Beer Kitchen has five different locations in Ohio and Indiana and offers a seasonal menu with delicious, handcrafted comfort food. I get something different every time I go, but I'm never disappointed.

Roosters

Roosters has been my family's go-to for years. It's a fun sports bar where you can always expect crispy fries and hearty wings. I order the same thing every time: boneless wings with medium sauce and a side of curly fries.

Bibibop

Bibibop serves healthy and affordable Asian food. You can customize your bowl to exactly how you want it. I like to get rice, chicken, potatoes, plenty of vegetables, and yum yum sauce on mine.

Brassica

Brassica serves sandwiches on housemade pita or salads with lentils and rice. You can customize your order with a variety of options, such as falafel, chicken, brisket, hummus, and many scrumptious vegetables. I recommend getting their fries too!

BJ's Restaurant & Brewhouse

With an incredibly large menu, there's something for everyone at BJ's. But the main reason I go is to get their delicious pizookie dessert: a warm, oven-baked pizza cookie with ice cream that melts over the top. My stomach's growling thinking about it!

Mac & Joe's

Mac & Joe's has a special place in my heart as it's located right in my college town of Oxford, Ohio. It serves the best bar food, offering hamburgers, sandwiches, wings, and more. Ask for their tater tots and spicy aioli on the side!
