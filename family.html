My Family

Where to even begin! My family is everything to me. I'm beyond grateful to come from such amazing, supportive parents and to grow up with my sister who is like a best friend to me. From dinners around the kitchen table to walks in the neighborhood, my family has always been super close. I owe a lot to them for becoming who I am today.

My dad is goofy, handy, supportive, caring, and incredibly smart. I always go to him when I need advice, help, or just some encouragement. My dad is also generous; everything he does is for his family. He has provided me with such a wonderful life and I am so grateful.

My mom is gentle, understanding, beautiful, warm, and kind. I enjoy spending time with her, even if it's just running errands to Walmart and Tim Hortons. My mom cares deeply for everyone in our family and she's extremely thoughtful. She always puts us before herself. I also can't forget to mention that she is one of the best cooks I know. My mom always lets me know how proud she is of me and it means the world.

My sister is funny, athletic, sharp, mature, and humble. She can be stubborn at times, but she's always fun to be around. Syd and I grew up doing everything with each other, and she knows me better than anyone. We always have a good laugh when we're together and share many wonderful memories. I love watching her grow into the incredible person she is. I am so proud to be her sister.
